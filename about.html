Travel

I love the idea of exploration and adventure. The excitement of discovering new places, getting immersed in different cultures, and experiencing the unfamiliar is such an amazing feeling. Travel allows me to satisfy my curiosity about the world, providing endless opportunities to learn and widden my perspective.

Beyond the adventure, travelling offers me significant personal growth and self-discovery. My biggest recommendation to anyone who asks about travel is that they must go on a solo trip at least once in their lifetime. Of course, pick a foreign country where you are unfamiliar with yet can also find a sense of familiarity. For example, if you have been in the US your entire life - you can visit Canada, where the primary language spoken is also English.

I made it an effort to visit a new destination at least twice a year. Sometimes life gets in the way and I don't get to go anywhere but luckily I have my other hobbies to keep me afloat.
